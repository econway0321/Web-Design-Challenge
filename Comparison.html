Latitude Comparison

Maximum Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of the year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due tot he tilt of the earth at the time of the year the data was gathered.

Humidity

The data presented provides humidity levels consistant throughout the nothern and southern hemispheres. I find this interesting as the hemispheres have opposite seasons.

Cloudiness

The percent of clouds is evenly dispursed across the nothern and souther hemispheres. With the cluster of cities at 0% I question if data was available for analysis.

Wind Speed

Having grown up in windy Wyoming, I am fasinated by cities whose wind speed is less than 10 mph. The wind speeds are relatively dispersed throughout the norther and southern hemispheres, with extreme wind occuring in the nothern hemisphere (could this be Wyoming??).
